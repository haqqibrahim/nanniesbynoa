Enthusiastic, knowledgable, empathetic, a finger on the pulse, ability to stay ten steps ahead, eyes in the back of your head, proactive, and playtime expert? You’re in the right place. We are always seeking City Savvy Nannies for United States’s beloved families. Apply today and begin the first steps towards finding your dream family.

Applicant Requirements:

  • Be at least 21 years of age.
  • Have a minimum of 5 years of private in-home childcare experience.
  • Provide phone and email information for 2 recent childcare employment references.
  • Provide current US work authorization for W2 employment.
  • Able to make a minimum 1-year commitment.
