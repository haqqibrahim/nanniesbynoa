Hire a Nanny

Making the lives of busy United States parents easier.

As a dedicated childcare agency, our full attention and singular focus is on providing the bespoke childcare your family needs, from part-time and temporary nannies to full-time live-in support, with providers specialized in learning & development, newborn care, and beyond.

We don’t believe parents should have to choose between what’s easy and what's best.

We serve families with the highest standards for their children’s care – often seeking caregivers with higher education, specialty training, and second languages, but always seeking flexibility, adaptability, and commitment.

Why NbN?

The benefits of working with our agency:

  • Rigorous 3-part vetting process

  • Federal level background checks
  • Verbal employment references

  • Time saving searches

  • A dedicated placement coordinator throughout your search

  • Quality over quantity – bespoke matching

  • Tips and resource offerings throughout your search

  • Expert guidance before, during & after placement

  • Industry experts with current market knowledge

  • Guidance in defining work agreements to incentivize for longevity

  • Payroll assistance with our trusted partners

  • Replacement protection

Noa Mintz

Founder

Noa Mintz founded Nannies by Noa in 2011, when she was only 12-years-old. You can imagine the acclaim that such a young entrepreneur has earned: segments on NBC’s Today Show and CNBC’s Squawk Box, interviews with Ryan Seacrest and Steve Harvey, a BBC Radio profile, feature stories in Fortune, United States Post, Forbes, United States Magazine, Time, Teen Vogue, and Entrepreneur.

Noa recently stepped away from the spotlight here to focus on her own higher education – she’ll be part of the Brown University Class of 2023, graduating with a degree in Cognitive Neuroscience. Go Bears!

Jo Barrow

CEO

Jo Barrow knows her way around Childcare: more than three decades of noteworthy experience has elevated her to a position of knowledge & leadership within the sector.

Jo is the 2013 Nanny of the Year awardee from the International Nanny Association (INA), recognized for her contributions to the in-home childcare industry. Three years later, she earned a seat on the INA Board of Directors.

Throughout her career as a nanny, a European-trained butler and Children’s Etiquette Consultant, Jo continues to drive positive industry change with participation in numerous programs that focus on the development, health, and safety of children. Working in some of America’s most prestigious homes, Jo has served as a consultant, caregiver, family assistant, and household manager.

Away from the office, Jo shines on another stage: she’s the proud stepmom to four super kiddos in Nyack, NY.

Lucy Monge

Recruitment Director

Lucy brings calm to our operational storm, where she oversees the recruitment of exceptional quality nannies into our network. She brings over 10 years of varied childcare experience to her role at NbN and has also earned her Nanny credential from the International Nanny Association and trained as a Foundational Newborn Care Specialist through Newborn Care Solutions. Lucy has also earned her BS in Vocal Performance. Her favorite role was playing Sam-I-Am in Kapilow’s rendition of Green Eggs and Ham, the operetta. She has a couple of associates degrees as well in music education and secondary education. Lucy is passionate about discovering talented nannies who are creative, thoughtful, and knowledgeable about childcare. She believes the best interviews are those where there is a free-flowing exchange of ideas. Whether she’s singing, nannying or interviewing, she brings fun into the mix!

Liza Monge

Admin & Communications

Liza works closely with our nannies, from reviewing applications to conducting interviews, taking references, providing professional support, and much more. Liza has 10+ years of professional childcare experience and has a special place in her heart for 3- and 4-year-olds. Liza earned her BA in Media, Culture and the Arts from The King’s College in NYC and earned a couple of associate degrees along the way including business administration and music performance. She’s continued her studies having earned her Foundational Newborn Care Specialist certificate as well as her 200-hour yoga instructor certificate. In her off time, she loves to play with her beautiful Mini Australian Labradoodle, Meadow. Here at Nannies by Noa, Liza is inspired by her belief that our families are some of the best parents in the world by making certain their children have the best possible care.

Rosie Moreno

Recruiting & Placement Lead

Rosie assists in the process of recruiting outstanding nannies into our network. With 14 years of experience in recruitment, she has developed a keen eye for identifying top talent across a range of industries. She holds an associate degree in kinesiology and will soon complete her training as a Foundational Newborn Care Specialist. During her free time, Rosie enjoys maintaining an active lifestyle and indulging in reading various genres. Above all, she cherishes spending quality time with her family and close friends. As a mother of a 13-year-old herself, she fully appreciates the importance of sourcing and thoroughly vetting nannies who can provide exceptional care for your children. Rosie brings a fresh and valuable perspective to our recruitment process as a result. Families can trust that their search for the ideally qualified nanny is in capable hands with Rosie at the helm!
